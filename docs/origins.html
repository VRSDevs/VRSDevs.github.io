Origins

PC multiplayer


Description

In the distant past, there were four types of element races: wind, fire, ground, and water. For centuries, they lived harmoniously until the dark matter was stolen by the ground.
As one of these cats, you must steal dark matter from your enemies in order to win the game.

Multiplayer offline game
