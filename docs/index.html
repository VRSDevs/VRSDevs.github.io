Know us

Exactly who are we?

We are a small team of indie game developers who love what we do. We put our heart and soul into every project we work on in order to create the best games possible. We are best known for our games such as XO Rivals or Jadoraki.



Team coming together: how did it happen?

Virtual Ravens was formed in 2019, where we made our first PC game: Origins. Over time, we developed for game jams until finally we decided to start working with virtual reality. Escape! with the game first introduced us to this world, but it wasn't until Jadoraki that we fully immersed ourselves in virtual reality.
