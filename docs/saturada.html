SATURADA

Z Game Jam


Description

A game called Saturada was proposed for the Z jam whose theme was 'from less to more'.

It follows the life of Lexa, an office worker who views life in black and white. However, because she tried to break away from the routine, her life took on the color of his decisions.

It will be up to you to make Lexa's decisions and change her life.
